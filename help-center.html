Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is PocketPal MiniFin?

PocketPal MiniFin is a user-friendly financial management tool designed to help you manage your personal finances effectively. It offers a range of features to track your income, expenses, savings, and investments.

2. How do I get started with PocketPal MiniFin?

To get started with PocketPal MiniFin, simply sign up for an account on our website or download our mobile app from your app store. Once you've created an account, you can start adding your financial information and setting up your budget.

3. Is PocketPal MiniFin free to use?

Yes, PocketPal MiniFin offers a free basic plan with essential financial management features. We also offer a premium subscription with advanced features and additional support options.

4. Can I connect my bank accounts to PocketPal MiniFin?

Yes, you can link your bank accounts and credit cards to PocketPal MiniFin to automatically track your transactions and account balances. We use secure encryption to protect your financial data.


5. How does PocketPal MiniFin help me budget my expenses?

PocketPal MiniFin allows you to set up a budget and categorize your expenses. It tracks your spending in real-time and provides insights into your spending habits, helping you stay on track and save money.
